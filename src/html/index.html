Frequently asked questions.

We are always available to answer your queries in detail, however we have answered the questions we get the most, in case you also have the same queries

How are each of these services priced?

The services are priced depending on the size and state of your stack, the number of users you have and the type of commitment/involvement you require from our team.

We can provide dedicated specialists, working full time with your team to deliver the best results or experts and engineers to help you on an hourly basis.

Pricing is derived by doing a preliminary diagnosis of your stack and aligning on the scope and timelines of the engagement.

Do you just do consulting and strategy building?

We are NOT a consulting firm and we take pride in it. We are an implementing firm who is output focused and believes in taking end to end ownership of their work. We will make strategies with you and implement them ourselves and keep experimenting until we crack the problem.

What stacks can you work with?

Our work is completely stack agnostic and we can work with any and all the stacks. We work on first principles.

We know how to drive a car, now if it’s a Ferrari or a Hyundai, doesn;t really matter, we get the job done.

PS. All our professionals are certified with the best tools out there including Mixpanel, HubSpot, MoEngage, Amplitude, Google, Omnisend, Klaviyo, Iterable and many more.

I have a growth team or at least means to do the same in-house, so why should I hire you?

Having an in-house growth team means you have reached a certain stage and traction and now require exponential growth. At this stage, deep vertical expertise is the most crucial aspect of your growth strategy.

Our leaders come with 10+ years of experience, most of which they have spent honing their skills in just one aspect of growth.

For example, if you are facing challenges with retention, our retention experts will be able to augment your growth team and guide them towards the fastest path to success.
