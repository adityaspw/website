Oneistox

Oneistox wins at making an automated sales engine in Edtech

Thanks to HubSpot and its frictionless implementation. From customer onboarding to winback, first call to sales close, and first interaction to omni channel marketing touchpoints - Oneistox delivers contextual user experience with HubSpot

{{> angles/angle-top-end}}

OneistoX is an ed-tech platform which provides cohort-based architecture courses to students with an aim to bridge the gap between academia and industry. With global architecture leaders as their mentors, Oneistox provides a platform for the student to connect with the leaders and learn from them in a cohesive and community led environment.

Oneistox was driving their sales and marketing via HubSpot but was failing to understand the behaviour of the customers in the sales funnel, and thus limiting their sales experiments and feedback loop. The team was also struggling to engage users once the user dropped out of the funnel and nurture them for the next cohort.

With no active feedback on sales experiments and marketing efforts, Oneistox was unable to earn ROI on their HubSpot setup

The challenge here is to collect data at different stages of the funnel with higher accuracy and devising nurturing and winback strategies on top of the data to keep them engaged even after they leave the funnel. After understanding the use case, Springwood team suggests a lead journey, entirely driven by HubSpot, which includes:-

  • Collect the lead using lead magnets and links across the interaction points (Email, Call, WhatsApp, Website, Social Media)
  • Onboard the visitor into the OneistoX ecosystem, giving them a glimpse of things they offer (Marketing automation)
  • Basis the lead interactionwith the website and CTAs across touchpoints, lead intent is being captured and customer communication is modelled accordingly
  • Low intent leads are being sent out a HubSpot sequence with information on Oneistoxâ€™s impact and success stories to build the intent
  • If a customer moves out of the funnel, engagement communications being sent to keep them updated about the course update and cohort calendar

With automated sales processes and engagement strategies configured in HubSpot with intelligence on user persona behaviour, Oneistox team has to do just one thing which they are best at - Close the deal and make their customers happy!
